Syd Barrett

Roger Keith "Syd" Barrett (January 6, 1946 - July 7, 2006) was a British singer. guitarist, composer, poet, artist and actor. Best known as the leader and founder of the English band Pink Floyd, who was later forced to leave the band due to mental illness caused by complications from taking severe drugs.
